About the Event

Overview

Peravoor Sports Foundation warmly invites you to take part in the Walkaroo Peravoor Marathon – 2025 (10.5K), a thrilling run through the evergreen hills of Peravoor, along winding roads sprinkled with surprises, and set in the soothing lap of nature.

Feel the crisp, unpolluted air filling your lungs, while the cool morning breeze brushes across your face. The sparkling streams flow alongside you as loyal companions, as you absorb the untouched beauty of the valley.

Escape from the noise, dust and endless congestion of city life, and let the flourishing greenery and vibrant wildlife fill your senses with peace and joy.

Experience Peravoor

The home of sparkling rivers and lush hills

The 7th edition of the Peravoor Quarter Marathon is proudly hosted by the Peravoor Sports Foundation together with several sports organizations and countless passionate supporters of sports.

With assistance from government offices, local councils and numerous legendary Sports stars across the nation, the event highlights the deep sporting spirit of this village. More than 5,300 participants joined last year’s edition, and this season we expect around 7,500 enthusiastic runners to take part.

🌱 Our Mission: Say No to Drugs

With the theme "Say No to Drugs," we emphasize that every single consistent step has lasting value. By running with us, you add strength to the cause—helping create awareness against addiction, while promoting fitness, positivity and a meaningful lifestyle.

Why Join Us?

Why participate?

We believe that every single person and every single step can make a difference! By participating you will help create awareness against drugs, promote a healthy lifestyle and culture of sports & fitness. More than that if you bring your family and children along, you will be contributing towards building a future where the next generation takes cognizance of these issues and participates in negating the bad ones and promoting the good ones.

✊

Fight Against Addiction

Create active awareness against drugs. Your registration and presence strengthen the message across communities, fighting drug abuse.

💪

Fitness & Health

Promote a healthy lifestyle and build a culture of sports and running. Experience the natural hill trails that test your endurance.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Build the Next Gen

Bring your children and family to participate in negating the bad influences, inspiring youth to take sports and active fitness early on.
